Web Application Name:
Formula 1 Then & Now

Specification:
 - LogIn & Register
 - Form(create, edit, details)
 - Gallery 
 - Rating
 - Statistics & History

Requirements:
 - users can create a profile 
 - users can add comments about one race and rate it
 - users can see some statistics over years about F1 and races
 - users can see some photos from different races and seasons
 - users can see all pilots and vote for their favourite or give him a note
 - users can see the rankings for pilots and teams
 - users can see details about a race that took place (qualifications, line up, some incidents from race, overtaking, winner and podium)

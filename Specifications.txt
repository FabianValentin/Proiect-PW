Web Application Name:
Formula 1 Then & Now

Specification:
 - LogIn & Register Forms
 - Gallery 
 - Rating Pilots

Requirements:
 - users can see some photos from different races 
 - users can see all pilots and vote for their favourite or give him a note
 - users can see the rankings for pilots after votes
 - users can see details about a race that took place (qualifications, line up, some incidents from race, overtaking, winner and podium)
